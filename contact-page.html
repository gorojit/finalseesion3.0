Contact us for Plastics

We buy Plastics at Rs 5 per KG

Plastic collection is an essential step in managing plastic waste and ensuring it gets recycled appropiately. Here are the main types of plastic we accept.

  • PET(Polythene Terephthalate): Used for beverage bottles and food containers.
  • HDPE(High Density Polythene): Found in milk jugs and detergent bottles.
  • PVC(Polyvinyl Chloride): Common in pipes and vinyl products.
  • LDPE(Low Density Polythene): Used for plastic bags and cling wrap.
  • PP(Polypropylene): Found in food containers and bottle caps.
  • PS(Polystyrene): Used for disposable cutlery and foam cups.
  • Others (Eg., Polycarbonate): Used in multi-layered containers and specialty items.

Contact us for Metals

We buy Metals at Rs 14 per KG

Metal collection is a vital part of recycling processes aimed at reducing environmental impact and conserving natural resources. Here are the main types of metals we accept.

  • Iron: Used in construction, machinery and automotive industries.
  • Copper: Used in electrical wiring, electronics, and plumbing.
  • Lead: Used in batteries, radiation shielding, and as a protective coating for cables.
  • Aluminium: Used in packaging, aerospace, and transportation industries.
  • Any types of metals etc: Used for a variety of purposes.
